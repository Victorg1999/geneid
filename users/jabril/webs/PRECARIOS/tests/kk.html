What is AGAVE?

AGAVE (Architecture for Genomic Annotation, Visualization and Exchange) is an XML format developed by DoubleTwist, Inc. for managing, visualizing and sharing annotations of genomic sequences.

AGAVE is freely available to the life sciences community as an open standard to:
•Encourage the development of tools that manipulate, visualize and store genomic data
•Facilitate the exchange of genomic information
•Support system and data interoperability
     AGAVE Community
  • BioTools
  • DoubleTwist
  • SUN Microsystems
  • Weizmann Institute of Science
Organizations interested in joining the AGAVE community can send e-mail to info-agave@agavexml.org.
